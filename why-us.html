Why US

The foremost questions in your mind will be like Why us? What extra do we have to offer? How different are we? Are we really any different? What difference will it make to get a job from here? How can we make your life better? How true are we to our words?

Well, if you have these kind of questions in your mind, you are definitely not a robot! We don’t need a captcha for that! It is highly imperative and natural to have these questions at the back of your mind. And you should be expecting satisfactory answers to fill your appetite.

Finally, here is what we have to offer:

Job Guarantee:

Yes! You just read it right. We do guarantee a job if you pass our test. For more information spare a moment to visit our FAQ section.

Multiple Jobs:

Sounds unbelievable? Time to believe it. Thinking how is that possible? Here is how. The BPO industry is a fast paced industry. It has the highest attrition rate and inevitably the highest recruitment rate. Most of the time there are vacancies in multiple organizations. If you clear an interview of any one organization, it is highly likely that you would clear a few others. Thus it funnels down to a situation where you have cleared multiple interviews and you are the one choosing a job not the recruiters choosing you as a probable candidate. Still have a nano-doubt in your mind, share us your contact and we will share the story of those who have been there.

Providing the best option available:

It is normal to have vacancies in multiple organization at the same point of time. How do you choose where to go? That’s where we come in. We will give you the best option suiting your needs and caliber.

Six months of peace:

Once you get a job through us, you will be protected! You will be protected for six months starting your date of joining. If the unfortunate event of a process getting ramped down happens during this time, we will be happy to give you up to 50% of your in-hand salary for one month. This will take care of your basic expenses till the time you get another job. When it comes to finding another job, we are here! We will get you aligned with interviews and will try our best that you get a job again depending on your eligibility.

Congratulatory Gift:

We feel proud when you get a job through us. Hence we make sure you get a gift from us for making us feel proud.

Referral Bonus:

For every candidate referral, the referrer gets a four figure bonus amount depending on the referee’s selection and sustainability. Here’s how it works. You have a friend or an acquaintance who needs a job. You share his/her contact with us. We make sure that your friend or acquaintance gets a job and in the process you get the referral amount provided your referee meets the terms and conditions. You can refer someone for an entirely different organization and still you can enjoy the fruits of your referral.
