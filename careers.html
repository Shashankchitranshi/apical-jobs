Careers

We are always in the lookout for smart and committed people who can associate with us and add value to our mission. We don’t shy away from giving opportunities. We are in favor of those who have the ability to take ownership and deliver on one’s own accord. Check out this page for the current offerings.

Business Development (Customer Segment) Intern:

About the internship:

We are seeking people with social media presence. It is a virtual internship whereby we offer you to do the internship from the comfort of your home. You will be required to refer job-seekers to us for the profiles we give based on our client requirements. You need to find job seekers for specific profiles and share their contact with us. You will be trained how to perform the job. Moreover, there are monetary benefits too. The monetary benefits depends on the candidates you refer. In short, hunt for job seekers. Happy hunting!

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Anyone with excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Hands on with Microsoft Excel
  • Active social media presence
Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Searching potential candidates as per client requirements and share their data with us
  • Preparation of comprehensive report on target profiles
  • Follow up with potential candidates
  • Interview scheduling and follow up
Perks
  • Work at your comfort
  • You can leverage the social network of your friends and family and monetize it
  • You will take decisions on your own, no one to boss around
  • There is no limit to the amount of stipend you can earn.

Stipend : Pay is based on performance.

Duration : At least 3 months. It can be extended based on performance. There is no specific start date. You can start when you think you can.

Interested candidates are to mail at hr@jobspah.com attaching their resume with subject Business Development (Customer Segment) Intern and a short SOP.
