ABOUT US

Apical Jobsurance Pvt Ltd is a recruitment and consultation firm registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, on 10th of May, 2018. Essentially we are a start-up and growing at a healthy speed. We are the bridge between job seekers and the firms looking for suitable candidates. The founder of the company is Dipan Sen, a student of the Indian Institute of Management (IIM). Presently we have three directors. Two of us are pursuing MBA and the third is an assistant professor. We have a number of interns and freelancers working for us.
